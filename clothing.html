Palestine Solidarity Clothing Brands

Boycotting brands and their Alternatives

TOMMY HILFIGER

Tommy Hilfiger, a prominent brand under the umbrella of PVH, the parent company, has joined in the efforts, demonstrating a commitment to supporting israel amidst the crisis

VOY

VOY is an egyptian clothing brand that stands with palestine during these troubling times by specifying part of its profit as donations to gaza

American Eagle

American Eagle, a renowned American clothing and accessories retailer headquartered in Pittsburgh, recently sparked controversy by displaying the Israeli flag on their flagship billboard in Times Square, New York. This move was seen as a clear declaration of support for Israel in the ongoing conflict with Palestine.

Defacto

Defacto is a turkish clothing brand that is one of many that supports palestine and contributes a big part of the donations sent to gaza

Zara

On 24 October, Palestinians called for the boycott of the retail clothing chain Zara after its Israeli franchise holder hosted far-right Knesset member (MK) Itamar Ben Gvir for a campaign event
